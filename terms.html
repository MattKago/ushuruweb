About Ushuru Flow

Ushuru Flow is a software platform that helps Kenyan businesses prepare tax returns for submission to the Kenya Revenue Authority ("KRA"). The Service uses artificial intelligence and automation to extract transactions from invoices, receipts, spreadsheets, and other financial documents you upload, normalize them into a structured ledger, and produce KRA-compatible return files for filing via the iTax system.

The Service currently supports:

  • Value Added Tax (VAT) — monthly returns for VAT-registered businesses, including VAT3 export.
  • Turnover Tax (TOT) — monthly returns for businesses with annual gross turnover between KSh 1,000,000 and KSh 50,000,000 who are eligible under the Income Tax Act.

Additional tax types, including Withholding Tax (WHT), Pay As You Earn (PAYE), and Monthly Rental Income (MRI), may be added over time and will be subject to these Terms.
