You If a supplier didn't give me an ETR receipt, can I still claim input VAT on the invoice?
AI No. Under Section 17(3) of the VAT Act, input VAT is only deductible against a tax invoice from a registered supplier issued through an ETR/TIMS-compliant device. Without a valid ETR receipt the input VAT cannot be claimed — even if the underlying invoice is genuine.VAT Act §17(3)eTIMS Guide p.12
